Happy Birthday Harold B. Lee!

Born on March 28th, 1899 in Cliffton, Idaho with 5 siblings was none other than a Harold Bingham Lee. Harold B. Lee was called to the Holy Apostleship on the 10th of April, 1941. He became the President of the Quorum of the Twelve and First Counselor to the First Presidency on the 23rd of January, 1970. On the 7th of July in 1972 he was called to be the 11th President of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, following the death of Joseph Fielding Smith.
